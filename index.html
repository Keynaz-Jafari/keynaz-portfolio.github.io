About Me

I’m a Computer Engineering undergraduate at Shiraz University, passionate about blending software engineering with user-centered design. I work across full-stack web (React, Django), embedded systems (AVR/Arduino), and UI/UX prototyping (Figma, Rive), turning complex ideas into scalable, intuitive experiences.

Beyond coursework, I’ve taught programming, mentored peers in a UI/UX lab, and contributed to IEEE events through technical work and creative storytelling (film & photography). My HCI interests focus on usable, inclusive systems that help people achieve more with clarity and delight.
